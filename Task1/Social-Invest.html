3 How It Works

The SIS Engine enables social investors
to make data-driven decisions with confidence across
finance and philanthropy.

The SIS Engine combines traditional relational databases with Large Language Models (LLMs) to enhance data accuracy, coverage, and adaptability. By continuously updating our taxonomy and methodology in response to shifts in materiality, we ensures that social insights remain relevant, measurable, and actionable. Generating both qualitative analysis and quantitative metrics, the SIS Engine enables social investors to make data-driven decisions with confidence across finance and philanthropy.
